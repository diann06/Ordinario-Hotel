SNOW CART

It is prepared with raw milk, vanilla and a touch of cinnamon, stored in a metal instrument to shape it and served on a piece of paper. It is the traditional ice cream called El Nile. An ice cream that has been prepared since 1940 to the delight of its consumers.



ECHEVERRIA TEATHER

The construction of this place began in 1833, by order of Governor Francisco García Salinas, to complement the obelisk erected in commemoration of the 23rd Anniversary of the Independence of Mexico, which is why it was originally named Teatro Hidalgo.


PARISH OF PURIFICATION

Its construction began in 1750, and finished in 1780. It has a tower on the south side, and it shows bullet holes, a product of the Mexican Revolution and the Cristero War.



ÁGORA MUSEUM

One of the main historical and architectural riches of El Mineral, Zacatecas; This property functioned as a School of Mining and Metallurgy Practice, a hospice, even a military barracks, evidencing over the years the political and economic situation of the municipality.


THE POTOSINE

La Potosina is one of the places that has become a meeting point for friends and families in El Mineral, it is a soda fountain that has existed since 1937. Where Fresnese children and adults visit the soda fountain daily, as a place of coexistence.
